---

## **Executive Summary**

### **Project Overview:**

The Ocean Lamp with LED Lights is a decorative and ambient lighting device designed to create a calming ocean-like atmosphere indoors. The lamp uses programmable LED lighting combined with a textured acrylic or resin body to simulate realistic water and wave effects. It produces soft, color-changing illumination that enhances relaxation and visual comfort.

The device operates through simple button control or remote-based adjustment, allowing users to change brightness, color modes, and lighting patterns. Designed with energy-efficient LEDs and a compact structure, the Ocean Lamp is suitable for bedrooms, living rooms, offices, meditation spaces, and hospitality environments.

This project emphasizes aesthetic design, energy efficiency, affordability, and emotional well-being through ambient lighting technology.
